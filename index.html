Just starting with your UnitedHealthcare plan? Whether it's activating your UCard, using your CareFlex benefits, or creating your online member account, you came to the right place. Use this quick checklist to get started today.

1. Activate Your UnitedHealthcare Member Account

  • Begin managing your health benefits online with a secure UHC member account.
  • Go to activate.uhc.com
  • Enter your Member ID
  • Create a username and password
  • Set up your security preferences
  • Start exploring plan details, costs, and provider options

Already a member? Sign in to myuhc.com

2. Activate Your UCard – Your Key to UHC Benefits

UnitedHealthcare UCard® is more than just your member ID. It gives you access to:

  • Prescription and OTC benefits
  • Dental vision, and hearing benefits.
  • Fitness and healthy food allowances (for some eligible plans)

To activate your UCard please go to activate.uhc.com or call the number on the back of the card.

3. Activate CareFlex – Flexible Spending Made Simple

If your employer or plan offers CareFlex, you can go to activatecareflex.uhc.com to:

  • Register your CareFlex Card
  • Check your balance and eligible expenses
  • View your transactions and submit reimbursements

Tip: Protect your CareFlex login information. It is used to manage your benefit funds.

Frequently Asked Questions- activate.uhc.com

Q: How do I activate my UnitedHealthcare member account?

A: Your account can be activated in just a few easy steps:

  • Visit myuhc.com or open the UnitedHealthcare® app.
  • Choose “Register” or “Activate Account.”
  • Enter the required personal details, such as, Member ID, date of birth, and ZIP code.
  • Create your username and password.
  • Verify your account with the security code that will be sent via your email or phone.

After your account has been activated, your plan, benefits and tools will be available for you 24/7.

Q: What if I lose my UCard?

A: If you have lost or stolen UCard, please follow these steps:

  • Sign in to your UHC account at myuhc.com or via the app.
  • Select the UCard section and select “Request Replacement”
  • Verify your shipping address and submit the request.

Or, you can contact the Member Services number on your plan documents and request a replacement card.

Q: Can I use the UnitedHealthcare app to find a doctor?

A: Absolutely! Here’s how to go about it:

  • Log in to the UnitedHealthcare app.
  • Tap "Find Care" or "Search for Doctors."
  • Input your location and type of provider (i.e., Primary care, Specialist).
  • Review the results and filter by distance, language, or rating. You can even call a provider or get directions to their location right from the app.

Q: What pharmacies can I use with my plan?

A: To locate a network pharmacy:

  • Access your account on myuhc.com or via the app.
  • Select "Pharmacy & Prescriptions".
  • Use the Pharmacy Locator Tool to find in-network pharmacies in your area. This way you can ensure that your prescriptions are covered at the least possible amount.
